Interviewer | Hostess | Content Creator

Hype, Professional and Quirky.

What else could you ask for?

Before Ovilee made her professional debut in front of hundreds of thousands on the LCS stage, she worked a variety of positions in the collegiate and high school esports scene. She founded her college’s Esports Club and led events for companies like Blizzard, Riot, and Yahoo, all before graduating from Chapman University. She majored in both Communication Studies and Broadcast Journalism and knows enough Japanese to understand the fight scenes of her favorite anime.

I’ve been in love with games since I picked up a N64 controller and ran around Kokiri Forest in Ocarina of Time. I was already a seasoned gamer when I hit high school but after being introduced to esports through Starcraft 2 and League of Legends I knew what I wanted to do with the rest of my life.


I’ve been working within the esports scene professionally for more than 6 years now and have done everything from Social Media Management to Broadcast Directing and Producing. But now I’ve found my dream job in creating content in front of a camera for a variety of games like: League of Legends, Teamfight Tactics, Shadowverse, Stardew Valley, Apex Legends, Rocket League, and more.


I can’t wait to share my world with you.
